# EVE Verified — Design Partner Agreement

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-05-25
**Between:** Organiq Sweden AB ("EVE Verified") and Design Partner ("Partner")

---

## 1. Purpose

This agreement outlines the terms of a design partner relationship for EVE Verified TPRM — Evidence Coverage v1. The purpose is to validate the product through real consulting use, with structured feedback from a domain expert, before general availability.

This is not a service agreement, subscription contract, or consulting engagement. It is a collaborative product validation arrangement.

---

## 2. What the Partner receives

During the design partner period, the Partner receives:

- **Access to EVE Verified TPRM** in its current state, including updates as they ship
- **All framework packs** available during the design partner period (initially: TPRM-critical SOC 2 / NIST 800-53 subset, plus EU AI Act demo)
- **Unlimited client/vendor workspaces** for testing and real engagement use
- **Local/offline deployment** when available
- **Excel and PDF export** of evidence coverage results
- **Direct access to the product builder** (Joakim Eklund) for feedback, questions, and scope discussion
- **No licensing fees** during the design partner period

---

## 3. What the Partner contributes

In exchange, the Partner agrees to:

- **Validate evidence rules:** Review 10–20 TPRM evidence rules for substantive accuracy. Confirm that evidence expectations, strong/supporting/negative terms, and result logic reflect real consulting practice.
- **Provide calibration material:** Supply 5+ representative evidence documents per control area (anonymized or synthetic) to test whether rules produce correct results against real-world evidence.
- **Give structured feedback:** Monthly written feedback on workflow, outputs, gaps, and accuracy. Format is flexible — written notes, annotated reports, or brief summaries. Async is fine.
- **Flag product issues:** Report bugs, usability problems, or incorrect results as they arise.
- **SME validation signature:** The Partner's validation is recorded in the evidence rule metadata (name, credentials, date, cryptographic signature). This makes the TPRM expertise traceable in every rule and report that references it.

---

## 4. Reference and case study rights

If the Partner becomes comfortable with the product:

- EVE Verified may reference the Partner as a design partner in future materials (with prior written approval of any specific reference text)
- EVE Verified may describe the design partner engagement in anonymized form (industry, use case, results) without naming the Partner, unless the Partner opts in to being named
- The Partner may reference EVE Verified in their own consulting materials at their discretion

No reference or case study use occurs without the Partner's explicit consent.

---

## 5. Confidentiality

- **Client evidence:** All client/vendor documents uploaded by the Partner during engagements are treated as confidential. EVE Verified does not access, retain, or use client evidence beyond the runtime evaluation described in the product scope. In local/offline mode, client evidence never leaves the Partner's environment.
- **Product details:** Pre-release product capabilities, architecture details, and roadmap shared during the design partner period are confidential and should not be shared publicly without agreement.
- **Feedback:** The Partner's feedback may be used internally to improve the product. Specific feedback is not attributed publicly without consent.

---

## 6. Disclaimer — not legal or audit advice

EVE Verified TPRM provides deterministic evidence coverage (Supported / Partial / NO_ANSWER). It does not provide:

- Legal advice
- Audit certification
- Compliance scoring or compliance opinions
- A substitute for professional auditor judgment

The Partner understands that v1 is an early-stage product under active development. Evidence rules are validated through the design partner process but are not guaranteed to be complete or error-free. The Partner is responsible for professional judgment in all client-facing use.

---

## 7. Design partner period

The design partner period begins on the date access is provided and continues for **six (6) months** unless extended by mutual agreement.

During this period, there are no licensing fees.

---

## 8. After the design partner period

At the end of the design partner period:

- The Partner may continue using EVE Verified under a **Consultant Pro license at 50% of standard pricing** for the first year after the design partner period ends
- Standard pricing applies from year two onward
- The Partner is under no obligation to continue after the design partner period
- If the Partner chooses not to continue, access ends and no further fees apply

Current standard Consultant Pro pricing will be communicated before the design partner period ends.

---

## 9. Termination

Either party may end the design partner arrangement at any time with written notice. On termination:

- The Partner retains any reports and exports already generated
- EVE Verified retains any feedback already provided
- No further obligations apply to either party
- Confidentiality obligations in §5 survive termination

---

## 10. Acceptance

This agreement is accepted when both parties confirm in writing (email or message is sufficient).
